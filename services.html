Free Medical Monitor

Our gym provides free medical monitoring to ensure that you are exercising safely. We track vital signs such as heart rate and blood pressure to keep your health in check while you work towards your fitness goals.

Free Beverage

Stay hydrated with our complimentary beverage service. Enjoy a variety of refreshing drinks before, during, or after your workout to keep your energy levels up.

Free Fitness Classes

Join our free fitness classes led by experienced instructors. We offer a range of classes from yoga and pilates to high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to suit all fitness levels.

Salt Water Pool

Relax and rejuvenate in our saltwater pool. Saltwater pools are gentler on your skin and hair, providing a luxurious and health-conscious swimming experience.

Variety of Equipment

Our gym is equipped with a wide variety of state-of-the-art machines and free weights. Whether you're looking to build muscle, increase endurance, or improve flexibility, we have the right tools for you.

Free Showers

After your workout, take advantage of our clean and spacious showers. Freshen up and leave the gym feeling as great as your workout made you feel.
