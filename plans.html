MUAY THAI

Muay Thai, also known as Thai boxing, is a combat sport and martial art that originated in Thailand. Known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," Muay Thai uses punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes to create a highly effective full-body workout. Classes typically include conditioning drills, technique training, and sparring sessions, helping to improve strength, agility, and self-defense skills. Whether you're training for fitness or competition, Muay Thai offers a dynamic and challenging way to stay in shape.
